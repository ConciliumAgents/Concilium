# Code of Conduct

Concilium welcomes focused, respectful collaboration around local agent tooling.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and kind.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and grounded in evidence.
- Respect privacy boundaries around local sessions, provider logs, and user memory.
- Assume mistakes can happen, and help repair them without personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private tokens, local session logs, account details, or user-specific memory.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion after maintainers ask to move on.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block users, or restrict participation when behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.
